QR SCAN SETUP GUIDE
====================

1. Print the signage template (qr-signage-template.svg) at A4 or 8x10. Laminate if the placard will live outdoors.
2. Generate a QR code that points to https://your-domain.com/qr-landing and drop it inside the "Place QR code here" square.
3. For multiple locations, append ?location=<slug> so the automation knows which store captured the visit.
4. Place the placard at eye level near the checkout counter or waiting area.
5. Test with both iOS and Android cameras to ensure the landing page opens within 1 second.
6. Update the review threshold under Settings → Review Funnel if your escalation rules change.
7. Share this document with franchise partners so they can reproduce signage without asking the CX team.
